About M & W Electrical Services

With over 20 years of experience, we are your trusted partner for professional electrical solutions across the UK.

Our History

Founded in 2003, M & W Electrical Services started as a small family business in Manchester. Today, we are a leading electrical service provider with a team of certified professionals serving clients nationwide.

Our Mission

We are committed to delivering safe, reliable, and innovative electrical solutions that exceed our clients' expectations while maintaining the highest standards of quality and professionalism.

Certifications

Our team is fully certified by NICEIC and accredited by TrustMark, ensuring compliance with UK electrical standards and regulations for every project we undertake.
